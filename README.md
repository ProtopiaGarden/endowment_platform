## About the concept

Protopia Garden is an attempt to substantially improve the structure of the endowment and close the gap between long-term thinking and short-term money. We redesign the endowment as digital and distributed — simultaneously supporting activities today and securing rights to the results of these activities over the long term.

Our solution is built on five interconnected engines:

1. **Endowment Platform** — radical reduction of costs for standard processes
2. **Programmable Relationships** — transforming endowment relationships into machine-readable objects
3. **Smart Contracts for Impact** — formalized second layer above the support flow
4. **Utility Endowment / Digital Endowment Principal** — rights to access digital infrastructure as part of the principal
5. **DAO Governance** — distributed decision-making mechanisms
